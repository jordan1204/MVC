Privacy Policy

eBsuccess recognizes the importance of protecting consumer privacy. The Web site is designed to give you as much control as possible over your personal information. In general, you can visit the Web without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. However, when you engage in certain activities on this site, we do need information to provide services that you request, and this statement of privacy explains data collection and use in those situations.

 

Collecting your Personal Information

We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (personal information) or allows us to contact you. Generally, this information is requested when you ask for free trial or ask us to forward information to you. We may ask you to provide certain information about yourself by filling out and submitting an online form. It is completely optional for you to engage in these activities. The personal information that we may require you provide us are such as your name, mailing address, e-mail address, and other personally identifying information. It is our intent to let you know before we collect personal information from you on the Internet.

 

Use of your Personal Information

We use the information you provide to us for purposes as below:

  • To correspond with you
  • To make the site easier for you to use
  • To help us create and deliver content most relevant to you

 

Control of your Personal Information

eBsuccess strictly protects the security of your personal information and honors your choices for its intended use. We will not share, sell or distribute your personal information to third parties for any reason. In addition, if you tell us that you do not wish to have this information used as a basis for further communication with you, we will respect your wishes.

 

Use of Cookies

Some of our web pages use "cookies". A cookie may be stored on your hard drive. It allows us to build a better web site by recording how and when guests use our site and to better serve you when you return to our site. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, however if you do, you might not be able to take full advantage of our sites.

 

eBsuccess websites may link to the other Web sites. Please note that eBsuccess is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these linked sites.
